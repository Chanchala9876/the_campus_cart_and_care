About Us

Campus Cart – A Trusted Marketplace for University Students

Introduction

Campus Cart is a student-to-student marketplace where university students can buy and sell second-hand items easily. Whether it's books, bicycles, laptops, furniture, or stationery, our goal is to help students get what they need at affordable prices and provide a platform to sell unused items conveniently.

Our Key Features

  • Exclusively for students – login using university email
  • Browse items by category – Books, Electronics, Furniture, etc.
  • Quick and easy posting – list your item in under 2 minutes
  • Search and filters – find what you’re looking for quickly

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can I sell items to students from other universities?

No. Only registered students with a valid university ID can buy or sell items on Campus Cart. The platform is exclusive to university students, and you must have a verified university email to access the marketplace.

2. Is it free to post items?

Yes, posting and browsing items is completely free for all students.

3. How can I contact the seller?

Click on an item to view the seller's contact info or message them directly (chat feature coming soon!).

4. What kind of items are allowed?

You can sell second-hand books, electronics, furniture, cycles, and stationery. Illegal items are not allowed.

5.How do I create an account

To create an account, simply sign up with your university email address. Once your email is verified, you can start posting and browsing listings.

6. How can I ensure safety while buying or selling?

Always meet in public or university-approved locations

Carry your university ID

Inform a friend or classmate before going to meet someone

7. How can I file a complaint?

Firstly, log in with your enrollment number. After logging in, click on the "Submit a Complaint" button. Then, fill in the necessary details about the issue and submit it.

8. What types of complaints can I file?

You can file complaints related to maintenance issues, cleanliness, safety concerns, hostel facilities, noise, or anything else that affects your hostel experience. We aim to address all types of grievances.

9. Is there a deadline for submitting a complaint?

There is no specific deadline for submitting complaints. You can file a complaint at any time, but the sooner you report an issue, the faster it can be resolved.

10. Who will see my complaint?

Your complaint is only visible to the hostel caretaker or authorized staff responsible for resolving such issues. If you choose to submit the complaint anonymously, your identity will not be shared with anyone.
